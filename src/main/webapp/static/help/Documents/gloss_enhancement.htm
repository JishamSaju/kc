Enhancement

A change to KC that improves its functionality in some way, either by adding functionality that does not exist in the baseline system (Coeus), or making substantive changes to functionality that does exist in Coeus.  Requests for enhancements from the KC SME groups must show benefit over cost, and are reviewed by the KC Technical Team and Functional Council.  The Functional Council ultimately votes to include, exclude, or defer enhancements in KC.

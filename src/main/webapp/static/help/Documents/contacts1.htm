Contacts

The Contacts page of the Award document has four tabbed sections that allow you to view, specify and maintain contact information for key personnel, unit, sponsor, and central administration individuals involved in the award.  It provides a single, convenient location for contact information of institutional and sponsor personnel with roles in award administration, including organization (or unit), relationship with the project, address, email, and telephone numbers.  Key Personnel must include at least one proposal and one award administrator.  Unit contacts are populated automatically based on Lead Unit, and Central Administration contacts are populated based on either Lead Unit or Sponsor.

Sponsor Template Sync:  In addition to viewing and maintaining contact information, you are also able to synchronize the Sponsor Contacts section with default data from a Sponsor Template that has been previously applied to the Award document.

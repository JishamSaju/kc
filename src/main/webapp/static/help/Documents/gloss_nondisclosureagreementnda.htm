Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

An agreement (a.k.a. secrecy agreement, confidentiality agreement, or mutual nondisclosure agreement) signed by two or more parties who agree to maintain specified confidential information for a specified period of time.  Agreement is typically subject to governing laws of the state or federal government.  Depending on organizational policy, agreement may be signed by the institutional official or other individual(s) officially designated by the parties to the agreement.

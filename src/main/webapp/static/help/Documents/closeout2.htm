Closeout

 

The Closeout section of the Subaward page is what you use to record information related to the closing out of a subaward.  It is in this section where you have the ability to specify dates (requested, followup, and received) and types of closeouts (reasons and/or actions for closing out a subaward).  Additionally, you are able to add textual comments related to each closeout type (multiple selection/entry lines may be added pertaining to a single subaward).

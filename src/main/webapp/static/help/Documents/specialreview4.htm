Special Review

The Special Review page of the Award document is used to specify details of and obtain reviews and approvals from other institutional users or groups.  It utilizes common line item functionality for selection and entry of information in fields and then gives you the ability to add that information as a numbered line item.  Comment entry is also permitted.  The status update allows you to keep track of special reviews that are important to the administration of the award.

Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance

A catalogue published twice a year which describes domestic assistance programs administered by the Federal government.  This government wide compendium of Federal programs lists projects, services, and activities which provide assistance or benefits to the American public. A number assigned by the US Government to a particular federal agency and funding program. All federal grants have a CFDA number assigned based on the particular federal agency and funding program providing the grant. See also CFDA Reference.

Group

 

The Group document is an administration maintenance document used to designate a group of users who share a common business function and thus have common e-doc routing requirements.  In a nutshell, groups allow you to associate persons, roles or other groups with each other in order to assign the same role to all group members.  E-docs can be routed at any point in their approval process to either individuals or groups.  Group approvals may require approval of all group members or any one member.

Groups are integrated with the KEW system to manage workflow and document routing. They also control who can initiate certain types of restricted KC actions. This functionality is used to uniquely define groups that contain members who are KC users. Since e-docs can be routed to both individuals and workgroups, existing groups can be selected by users to request action be taken on e-docs by their members.  Routing rules facilitate how groups are to be used. The business rules that govern the routing of e-docs to groups are configurable by your institution.

Business Rules and Routing Process

•      Anyone can initiate a Group document.

•      For the ‘Default’ (or blank) type workgroup, the document does Workflow Document Routing.

•      Blanket approval is permitted only by a user that belongs to the WorkflowAdmin workgroup.
